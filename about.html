About Me


Hello, everyone! My name is Neeraj Kumar Joshi, and I'm a passionate 22-year-old front-end developer. In 2021, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Applications from Amrapali Group of Institutes in Haldwani. Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications, which allows me to deepen my knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

During my academic journey, I have acquired a strong foundation in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am continually exploring new frameworks and libraries to stay up-to-date with the latest trends in front-end development.

My goal is to secure a challenging position where I can apply my skills and creativity to contribute effectively to the growth of an organization and, at the same time, nurture my personal and professional growth. I believe that every project is an opportunity to learn and improve, and I'm always excited to take on new challenges that push my boundaries.
